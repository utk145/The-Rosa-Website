Our Story

The Rosa is a legendary coffee restaurant and bar in Farringdon's Exmouth Market, with over a century of rich history. Our cozy atmosphere and delicious food have made us a beloved gathering spot for locals and tourists alike. Come join us for a cup of coffee, a hearty meal, or a refreshing cocktail, and experience the unique and authentic traditions of Frankfurt's culinary culture at The Rosa.
